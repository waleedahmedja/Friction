# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026  
Last Updated: March 2026  

Friction (“the App”) is a commitment enforcement system designed to help users voluntarily maintain focus.  

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

---

## 1. Data Collection

Friction does **not** collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data.

The App does not collect:

- Personal identifiers  
- Contact information  
- Location data  
- Usage analytics  
- Advertising identifiers  
- Biometric data  
- Cloud backups  
- Keystrokes or screen content  

All focus session data is stored locally on your device using Android DataStore.

No data leaves your device.

---

## 2. Accessibility Service Usage

Friction uses Android’s Accessibility Service solely for session enforcement purposes.

Accessibility access is used to:

- Detect which application is currently in the foreground  
- Block selected apps during an active focus session  

Friction does **not**:

- Record keystrokes  
- Capture screen content  
- Monitor text input  
- Track browsing behavior  
- Transmit any accessibility data externally  

Accessibility data is processed locally and never transmitted.

Accessibility permission is required only for enforcing blocked apps during active sessions.

---

## 3. Device Administrator Usage (Hard Commitment Mode)

When Hard Commitment Mode is enabled, Friction may activate Android Device Administrator permissions.

Device Admin is used strictly to:

- Prevent uninstall during an active session  
- Prevent disabling enforcement mid-session  

Device Admin permissions are automatically released when the focus session ends.

The App does not use Device Admin for remote control, surveillance, or device management beyond session enforcement.

---

## 4. Biometric Authentication

If enabled, biometric authentication is used to confirm intentional session termination.

Biometric data is processed entirely by Android’s secure system APIs.  
Friction does not access, store, or transmit biometric information.

---

## 5. Data Storage

All data generated by the App:

- Is stored locally on your device  
- Is not transmitted to external servers  
- Is not shared with third parties  

Deleting the App removes all locally stored session data.

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

Friction does not use:

- Analytics SDKs  
- Advertising SDKs  
- Tracking frameworks  
- Cloud databases  

The App operates entirely offline.

---

## 7. Ethical Use Commitment

Friction is designed for voluntary self-discipline.

It must not be used for:

- Surveillance of others  
- Coercive behavioral control  
- Monitoring individuals without informed consent  

The App is intended solely for personal use.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

Friction does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.  
Since no personal data is collected, no special data processing occurs.

---

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect improvements or legal requirements.

Any changes will be reflected in the “Last Updated” date above.
